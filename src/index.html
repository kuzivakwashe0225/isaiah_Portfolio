About me

I am Isaiah Kuzivakwashe Chikeya, a solutions-oriented Computer Systems Engineering graduate from Midlands State University, Zimbabwe. I specialize in software development, embedded systems, and real-time standalone IoT solutions that solve real-world problems.

I’ve developed several innovative systems including a Chord Catcher a Raspberry Pi powered real-time guitar chord recognition device using FFT and audio signal processing and a Remote Prepaid Water Meter System with IoT integration, dashboard, and mobile app. I also engineered a Cattle Monitoring System using environmental sensors and real-time data analytics.

My stack includes React.js, Node.js, Next.js, Express.js, HTML/CSS, and Python. I’ve worked on enterprise dashboards and full-stack systems at Tuto Consulting and Lionturf Instrumentation & Calibration. I'm currently advancing my design and UI skills with Figma while pursuing certifications in Cybersecurity, Networking Basics, and Ethical Hacking.

Beyond tech, I’m a FIDE-rated chess player, rugby referee under Harare Rugby Referees Society, and an acoustic/lead guitarist. I love blending creativity with logic and am deeply committed to continuous learning and community-driven innovation.
